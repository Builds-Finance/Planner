🧠 Saved Bull Put Spread Strategy — Safety-First Professional Checklist

Market Environment Checks

  • Prefer neutral to mildly bullish market structure
  • Avoid strong downtrends
  • Prefer elevated IV percentile environments
  • Prefer setups after panic moves for theta decay + IV crush advantage
  • Avoid major event risk / binary events
  • Prefer stable large-cap stocks with strong liquidity and institutional participation

OI / Support Checks

  • Identify strongest institutional Put OI support zones
  • Ensure Put OI support is stable or building
  • Avoid aggressive Put OI unwinding
  • Choose short strikes ONLY below strong institutional Put OI support zones

Short Put Selection Rules

  • Prioritize survivability over premium collection
  • Preferred short put delta range: 0.15–0.22
  • Prefer far OTM strikes
  • Maintain 5–8% safety buffer from spot during high IV conditions
  • Avoid aggressive directional positioning

Hedge (Long Put) Selection Rules

  • Buy further OTM protective put
  • Preferred hedge delta range: 0.03–0.10
  • Hedge exists for:
    • tail-risk protection
    • defined risk
    • margin reduction
  • Keep spread width controlled and reasonable

Greeks Filters

  • Prefer very low gamma
  • Prefer positive theta
  • Prefer negative vega when possible
  • Avoid setups where gamma expands rapidly
  • Avoid short strikes where delta may quickly exceed 0.30

POP & Probability Rules

  • Prefer high probability setups
  • Target POP: preferably >70%
  • Favor consistency over high reward

Entry Rules

  • Enter only when:
    • price remains above support
    • institutional Put support remains intact
    • delta is within preferred range
    • gamma remains controlled
    • IV environment is favorable
  • Focus on entry quality to minimize future adjustments
  • Use controlled/small position sizing
  • Max loss per trade should remain manageable

Exit & Profit Booking Rules

  • Book profits at:
    • 50–60% of maximum profit
  • Do NOT hold till expiry unnecessarily
  • Prioritize smooth equity curve over maximizing final decay

Defensive / Exit Triggers

  • Exit or defend if:
    • short put delta expands above 0.30
    • major support breaks
    • Put OI unwinds aggressively
    • downside momentum accelerates
    • IV expands sharply during downside move

Core Philosophy

  • Capital preservation first
  • Minimize adjustments
  • Prefer high survivability setups
  • Accept lower reward for higher consistency
  • Focus on low-stress premium collection
  • Build smooth long-term compounding rather than aggressive returns
